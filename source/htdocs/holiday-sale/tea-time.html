Tea Time

“I’m late, I’m late for a very important date!” The March Hare is a charming character most famous for appearing, (however a bit tardy), in the tea party scene in Alice’s Adventures in Wonderland. “Tea Time” was the first in the series of bronze rabbits. There will only be 25 made in this very limited edition. All work is done with highest quality bronze pour, and in the ancient lost wax process.
