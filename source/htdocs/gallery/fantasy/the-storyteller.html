The Storyteller

It is the mission of “The Storyteller” to connect to his audience and help them see what he sees. He sets the stage and pulls the strings; he lifts the soul and instills hope, by stimulating the colors of your own imagination.

Inspiration for the piece came from by Michaels childhood years, living in the Lake District of England. He was fortunate to wander the magical countryside where the many great authors; Beatrice Potter, Lewis Carroll, J R Tolkien, Shakespeare, Sir Cannon Doyle and William Wordsworth also found inspiration.

Limited edition bronze, #8/10

Dimensions: 22”wide 44.5”tall, 20”deep, 118 pounds
